Othello Proposal

Project Page

Concept

Othello is a classic boardgame where to players place chips on a board. When an opponents chip is surrounded by your chips in some way it will be
flipped and become your chip. The 2 players take turns until the board is full. Whoever has the most chips at the end wins.

Genre

Othello is a fairly simple strategy boardgame.

Platform

This will be designed to be played on a computer.

Story

The story will be the classic you vs your mom. You may also choose another great rival of yours to face.

Aesthetics

The website will be designed pretty minimally. The focus will be on the gameplay on the board.

Gameplay

The player will play agains another person at the same computer as them. Both players will make choices on where to place a chip on their turn.
Their will be instructional pictures on the site to assist learning the game.
